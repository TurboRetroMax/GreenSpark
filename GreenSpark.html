Our Projects

Bioenergy

Bioenergy is a form of renewable energy derived from biomass, which is organic matter from plants and animals. It's a versatile energy source that can be used for heating, electricity generation, and transportation fuels. Biomass includes materials like wood, crops, crop residues, and organic waste.

Geothermal Power Plants in Urban Spaces

Urban environments can utilize two primary geothermal systems: Ground-source Heat Pumps (GSHP): GSHP systems capitalize on the ground's constant temperature. Deep Geothermal Systems: These systems tap into hotter underground areas.

Wind Energy-Optimized Drone Networks
